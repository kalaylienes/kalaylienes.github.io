About

Hey stranger,

It's NS - welcome to my little corner of the internet.

This is my personal blog where I plan to write about whatever I'm into...
...which may happen tomorrow, next month, or in true blog tradition: never again.

A very short origin story

  • born in ‘97, originally from Istanbul.
  • studied Computer Programming and after that Industrial Engineering Istanbul.
  • been living in Darmstadt/Germany for >2 years now.
  • currently in the thesis phase of M.Sc. in Informatics at TU Darmstadt.

What I do when work or academia lets me breathe

When I’m not busy with university or work stuff, you’ll probably find me:

  • trying (mostly failing) to improve playing metal stuff on electric guitar
  • messing around with music production in tiny steps
  • building random things with a 3D printer
  • or riding my road bike until my legs negotiate a peace treaty

If you're here for clean structure and regular updates - good luck.
If you're here for curious experiments and honest progress - welcome aboard.
